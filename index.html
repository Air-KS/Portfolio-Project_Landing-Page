Connecting stays, Creating memories

HollyConnect brings to life the experience of staying in a dream rental, providing immediate access to all essential information, from facility details to local activities, to make your stay perfect and hassle-free.

ABOUT

History

HollyConnect was born out of an airbnb experience:
the incessant search for information about where we're staying.

  • How do I reset the wifi?
  • how does this washing machine work?
  • Where are the house rules?
  • So many questions that sometimes end up in a call to the owner or frustration at a time when relaxation should be the order of the day.
  • Our question is simple: why is it so difficult to find this information?
  • Why isn't there a platform to provide a direct link between the organisation of the home and its occupant?
  • This reflection led to an idea that turned into an engaging application project, all as part of our end of 1st year project at Holberton School.
